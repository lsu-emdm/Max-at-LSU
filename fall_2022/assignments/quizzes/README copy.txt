How to play Music for Keys Ð

1. Press button labelled ÒGo!Ó, this will trigger the first event.
2. Place four fingers on keys 1-4 at the top of the keyboard. These fingers trigger the remaining events.
3. There should be 5-8 seconds in between key presses. The order of events is as follows: 1, 2, 3, 4, 3, 2, 1
4. Press f to stop
